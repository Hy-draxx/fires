Fire Safety and Educations

FIRE SAFETY

Fire safety reduces the risk of injury and building damage that fires can cause. Developing and implementing fire safety protocols in collages is not only required by but it is crucial to everyone's safety

RESIDENCE HALL SAFETY GUIDELINES

Cooking

  • Cook only in designated areas
  • Keep clutter away from cooking area
  • Never leave cooking unattended

Candles

  • Keep candlles in sturdy holders
  • Extinguish flame immediately afterusing
  • Never leave a burning candels unattended
  • Keep candles away from draperies and linens

Electricity

  • Always keep all socket switch off after use
  • Never leave anything plugged

How to Respond to Fire Alarm

Fire safety expert stress the importance of practicing escape plans in case of fire in a hostel.A fire alarm should never be ignored. Student must get out of the building immediately and stay out until given the direction to come back in.

FIRE SAFETY INSTRUCTION

If you Discover Fire or Smoke, Remember:R.A.C.E


  • Rescue: Remove anyone from immediate danger.
  • Alarm: Activate the nearest fire alarm pull station.
  • Contain: Close all doors to confine smoke and fire.
  • Extinguish/Evacuate: if the fire is small and you have been trained in the fire Extinguisher use,you can attempt to Extinguish a fire. Otherwise,follow your Evacuation plan.

If you catch fire do not run!

  • Stop where you are, and
  • Drop to the ground, and
  • Roll over and over to smother flames.
